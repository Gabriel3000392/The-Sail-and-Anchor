Our story

The Sail & Anchor is a Timaru favorite, family owned and proudly serving the community since opening in 1991. It’s a place where locals and visitors come together for great food, live entertainment, weekly Wednesday quiz nights, and good times. We’re passionate about local ingredients, community spirit, and creating a welcoming spot for everyone.

Fresh, local flavor

Our menu is built around seasonal produce, fresh seafood, and hearty pub classics.

Relaxed spaces

Whether you’re inside enjoying the lively bar or outside on our sunny terrace, there’s a spot for every occasion.

Entertainment & events

From live music nights to private functions, The Sail & Anchor is your go-to venue in Timaru. Don’t miss our quiz every Wednesday night.
